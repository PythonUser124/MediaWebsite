Social Media Guidelines

When creating your social media posts:

  • Keep messages clear and concise
  • Use compelling visuals that grab attention
  • Include relevant hashtags (e.g., #BBBCElection, #BikiniBottomVotes, #PattyParty, #KrabbyParty)
  • Maintain journalistic integrity and factual accuracy
  • Engage viewers with questions or calls to action
  • Tag candidates or campaigns when appropriate

Platform Tips

X (Twitter)

Perfect for breaking news alerts, quick updates, and poll results. Keep it under 280 characters for maximum impact.

Instagram

Use for visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes content, and infographics. Great for campaign event photos and candidate profiles.

TikTok

Ideal for short video clips from broadcasts, quick news summaries, and engaging with younger voters.
