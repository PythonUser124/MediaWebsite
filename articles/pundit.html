Instructions for Your Team

Pundits have big personalities and strong opinions. You can include pundit commentary in two ways:

This page can serve as the written op-ed version of your pundit segment. At least one pundit should have a brief op-ed on your website.

Pundit topics could include:

  • Campaign strategy analysis

  • Policy positions and their implications
  • Polling trends and what they mean
  • Campaign events and their effectiveness
  • Character and leadership qualities
  • Predictions for the election outcome

Alternative Format: Roundtable Discussion

If you choose to do a panel discussion instead of (or in addition to) an op-ed, structure it like this:

Anchor: [Opening question or topic introduction]

Pundit 1: [Response]

Pundit 2: [Agrees or disagrees, adds perspective]

Campaign Rep (optional): [Responds if a campaign representative is part of the panel]

Anchor: [Follow-up question]

Continue the discussion format. Pundits should respectfully disagree, debate, and offer different perspectives. Make it lively and informative!
