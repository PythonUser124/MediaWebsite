Following their scripted introduction speeches, presidential candidates from both the Patty Party and the Krabby Party faced rigorous questioning from the assembled press corps. BBBC reporters, along with representatives from interest groups and opposing campaigns, prepared comprehensive questions designed to challenge the candidates and reveal their positions, character, and readiness for office.

Below is our complete archive of questions prepared for the Patty Party candidates, SpongeBob SquarePants (President) and Patrick Star (Vice President). These questions range from softball inquiries designed to allow candidates to further their message, to hardball questions that put them on the spot, to argumentative questions that challenge their positions, and personal questions about their backgrounds.

Press Conference Questions for the Patty Party

Softball Questions

These questions allow candidates to further their message and present their campaign in a positive light.

General Questions

  • "How does your close friendship with each other make you two an effective leadership team?"
  • "How will the Patty Party bring citizens together in friendship and fun?"
  • "Your party talks of creating 'a more joyful land and ocean.' What does that joyful future look like to you?"
  • "Your platform emphasized compassion. How will compassion guide America's decisions?"
  • "Your party is dedicated to restoring unity. What gives you hope America can come together?"

For SpongeBob

  • "SpongeBob, as being someone who has earned minimum-wage your whole life, how has that connected you to everyday voters?"

For Patrick - Foreign Policy

  • China: "Your platform puts a lot of emphasis on peace and global collaboration. How will maintaining a friendly relationship with China help preserve world stability?"
  • Israel-Palestine: "Why does your party believe that the first step toward justice in Palestine is humanitarian aid?"
  • Russia-Ukraine: "Your platform rejects military escalation. How will peace talks protect Ukrainian lives, while also preventing nuclear conflict?"
  • North Korea: "How will maintaining pressure while also avoiding war keep citizens in the region safe?"

Policy Areas

  • Second Amendment: "How will gun rights and public safety be balanced by the Patty Party?"
  • Healthcare and Drug Policy: "What steps will be taken by the Patty Party to make healthcare more affordable for everyone?"
  • Abortion: "How will the Patty Party ensure safe and legal abortion access in Bikini Bottom?"
  • Education: "What is your plan to make all students have access to higher levels of education?"
  • Environment: "What policies will be implemented to protect ocean life and reduce pollution?"
  • Criminal Justice Reform: "What is the Patty Party's plan to reduce overcrowding in prisons?"
  • Immigration: "What will be your approach when it comes to helping immigrants become legal citizens?"
  • Nuclear Testing: "How will the lingering radiation issues be addressed by the Patty Party?"
Hardball Questions

These tough questions put candidates on the spot and test their knowledge of policy details.

General Questions

  • "Your party promises healthcare, education, environmental protection, and criminal justice reform. How do you plan on paying for this?"

For SpongeBob

  • "SpongeBob, some voters doubt your seriousness because of your bubbly personality. How do you prove you're ready to handle crises?"

For Patrick

  • "Patrick, critics say you lack experience‚Ä¶ in everything. How do you respond to concerns that you're unprepared to be vice president?"
  • "Patrick, there have been reports that you once forgot how to breathe. Voters want to know if they can trust you with executive orders."

Foreign Policy

  • China: "You condemn China's human-rights abuses, yet still plan to maintain an alliance with them. How do you justify partnering with a repressive nation?"
  • China: "If China continues expanding into the South China Sea or Taiwan, will you respond with consequences? If so, what will these consequences look like?"
  • Israel-Palestine: "Your platform calls for sanctions and embargoes to be placed on Israel. Are you prepared for the political and economic fallout sure to follow the punishing of a close U.S. ally?"
  • Israel-Palestine: "What will your next steps look like if Israel refuses to comply with investigations of war crimes?"
  • Russia-Ukraine: "Critics say that you are abandoning Ukraine by opposing giving them weapons. How do you respond?"
  • Russia-Ukraine: "Will you reconsider military aid if peace talks fail and Russia continues to invade and annex territory?"
  • North Korea: "As you said yourself, sanctions have barely any effect on North Korea. What concrete leverage do you have?"

Policy Areas

  • Second Amendment: "How do you respond to critics' claims that gun restrictions infringe on second amendment rights?"
  • Healthcare: "Government-funded healthcare is sure to be very expensive, how do you plan on paying for it?"
  • Abortion: "Some say abortion is morally wrong. Why does the Patty Party believe it still should be protected as a right?"
  • Education: "Many critics argue that 'pay it forward' programs unfairly burden graduates, how do you respond to these claims?"
  • Environment: "How do you address the claims that environmental regulations hurt businesses?"
  • Criminal Justice: "What is your response to claims that decriminalizing minor offences makes society less safe?"
  • Immigration: "Critics claim open borders create security risks, how do you address that?"
  • Nuclear Testing: "Many argue that nuclear cleanup is too expensive. Why should tax payers pay for it?"
Argumentative Questions

These questions directly challenge candidates' positions and abilities, requiring them to defend their stances.

For SpongeBob

  • "Your opponents say your optimism prevents you from making tough decisions. Isn't leadership more than just positivity?"

Foreign Policy

  • China: "You say that a war with China is too dangerous, but aren't we encouraging them to keep expanding their military and territorial claims by avoiding confrontation?"
  • China: "Is it not a national security risk to rely so heavily on Chinese imports?"
  • Israel-Palestine: "How can the U.S. force Israel to stand trial under international law when the U.S. itself is not signed to many of those courts?"
  • Russia-Ukraine: "Your party seeks to restore Crimea to Ukraine without the use of military pressure. What makes you think Russia would ever agree?"
  • North Korea: "Your platform relies solely on mitigating rather than subduing the effects and consequences of North Korea. Focusing on defensive technology does nothing to stop North Korea's human-rights abuses. Are you essentially accepting the status quo?"

Policy Areas

  • Second Amendment: "Do you think that gun violence is really reduced by background checks and licenses, or does it just limit law-abiding citizens?"
  • Healthcare: "There is a popular concern that free prescriptions could lead to overdose. How do you prevent that while at the same time keeping drugs accessible?"
  • Abortion: "What is your response to concerns that by providing abortion access, you are encouraging irresponsibility?"
  • Education: "Some believe vocational programs are undervalued. How will the policies of the Patty Party elevate them?"
  • Environment: "What is your justification for strict environmental protections when some argue that it is much too costly?"
  • Criminal Justice: "How will the Patty Party ensure that reducing sentences doesn't compromise justice for victims?"
  • Immigration: "How will the concerns of economic and social strain be balanced with your compassion for immigrants?"
  • Nuclear Testing: "Your platform has a heavy focus on addressing the aftereffects of nuclear testing. However, critics argue that there are other urgent issues in Bikini Bottom. How do you respond to those who think that your plan puts too much emphasis on nuclear remediation?"

These questions delve into the candidates' personal backgrounds and character.

For SpongeBob

  • "SpongeBob, what did you learn from failing your driving test 1,258,056 times?"

For Patrick

  • "Patrick, how does living under a rock affect your connection with everyday voters?"
